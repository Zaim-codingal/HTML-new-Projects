Why Rivers Are Important

Rivers supply fresh water for drinking, farming, and industries. They are also home to many animals and plants.

Uses of Rivers

  • Irrigation for Crops
  • Transport and Travel
  • Hydroelectric power

Quick Facts

  • Rivers flow from high land to low land.
  • Every river has a source and a mouth.
  • Floodplains are very fertile.
